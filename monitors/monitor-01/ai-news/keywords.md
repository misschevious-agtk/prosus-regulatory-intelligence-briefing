# Keywords — AI News

**Version:** v1.0 — 2026-05-13 — initial draft following Monitor 01 Block 2 / Block 3 initialisation. Quarterly review per `operating-preferences.md`.

**Purpose:** Retrieval terms feeding RSS, news API, and Google Alerts queries for the AI News domain. Designed for recall-favouring retrieval; the persona's interrogation checklist filters at the finding stage. Cross-references Monitor 02 `digital-markets-sector-state-aid/keywords.md` for AI × competition overlap (CMA AI Foundation Models work, DG COMP AI competition policy) — Rule 21 cross-monitor handling applies.

> **Header convention used in all monitor-level keyword files:** each tier is preceded by a short line stating what the tier is for. Inline `[broad]` markers flag noisy terms that need combination with another term. Inline `[FR]`, `[DE]`, `[IT]`, `[NL]` markers flag non-English entries. Multilingual variants are grouped with their English term rather than separated, because retrieval logic typically OR-combines variants of the same concept.
