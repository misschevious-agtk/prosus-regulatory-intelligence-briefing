# Keywords — Abuse of Dominance & Unilateral Conduct

**Version:** v1.0 — 2026-05-13 — initial draft following Block 2 / Block 3 initialisation. Quarterly review per `operating-preferences.md`.

**Purpose:** Retrieval terms for the Abuse of Dominance & Unilateral Conduct domain. This is the domain where academic-as-primary-signal weight is highest; Tier 2 is correspondingly long.

> **Header convention used in all four Monitor 02 keyword files:** each tier is preceded by a short line stating what the tier is for. Inline `[broad]` markers flag noisy terms that need combination with another term. Inline `[FR]`, `[DE]`, `[IT]`, `[NL]` markers flag non-English entries. Multilingual variants are grouped with their English term rather than separated, because retrieval logic typically OR-combines variants of the same concept.
