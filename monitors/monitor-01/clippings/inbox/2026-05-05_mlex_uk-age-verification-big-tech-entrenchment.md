( May 5, 2026, 12:05 GMT | Official Statement) -- MLex Summary: Expanding age-verification measures in the UK, as proposed by an ongoing child safety consultation, risks undermining privacy and fragmenting the open internet, a coalition of organizations including Mozilla, the Tor Project, Big Brother Watch and Open Rights Group has warned. In a joint statement Tuesday, signatories said that expanding age checks would cement the dominance of mainstream platforms, and that measures should instead address the root causes of online harm.
