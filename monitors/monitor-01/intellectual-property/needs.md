## Standing questions — carry-with-you, re-asked against every plausible item

1. **"Does this change the legal status of training data we use (including via Qwen) or output we produce (LCM, Toqan, Ailo, OLX Magic)?"**
2. **"Does it affect an IP licence we rely on — inbound (vendor terms, open-source) or outbound (any Prosus content we licence out)?"**
3. **"Does it create a new infringement theory we could be exposed to, or one we could assert against counterfeit and piracy actors?"**
4. **"Does it touch a vendor's IP indemnity we depend on, including the carve-outs and the high-risk-use exclusions?"**
5. **"Does it set precedent that reframes a previously settled assumption — fair use, TDM exception, computer-generated works, inventorship, joint authorship?"**
6. **"Does it implicate trade-secret exposure through employee use of AI tools, or through model-derived disclosure of confidential training inputs?"**
7. **"What does this mean for Tencent's IP position and for any Prosus group-level disclosure obligation flowing from it?"**
8. **"What is the marketplace-liability read across to OLX, eMAG, JET, Despegar, Swiggy — does this shift our notice-and-action posture or our trusted-flagger relationships?"**
9. **"Does this signal a deepfake or brand-impersonation pattern we should be enforcing against proactively rather than reactively?"**
