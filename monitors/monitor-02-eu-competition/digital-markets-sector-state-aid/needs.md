- Article 102 × DMA × DSA interplay — court rulings, agency speeches, academic literature, AG opinions on crowding-out or supplementation.
- FSR notifications, prohibitions, commitments, framework amendments, and the Commission's enforcement-pattern signalling.

  - Payments (PSD2 enforcement, PSD3 progression, interchange fee regulation, instant-payments rules, access to accounts and to payment systems).

  - OTA-sector consumer-protection × competition interactions affecting Despegar.
- State aid — Article 107 TFEU decisions, GBER amendments, IPCEI developments, recovery decisions, sectoral-aid frameworks.
- Market investigations and sector inquiries — CMA market studies, DG COMP sector inquiries, BKartA sector reports, CADE market studies, OECD competition committee deep dives, in food delivery, classifieds, OTA, payments, e-tail, e-pharmacy.
- Resilience, digital sovereignty, and strategic-autonomy policy developments — EU AI Continent Action Plan, EU Industrial Strategy, Apply AI Strategy, EU Data Union Strategy, Commission speeches and Council positions on digital sovereignty.
